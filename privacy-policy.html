Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Juvenis Medical LLC respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Name, email address, phone number, zip code, and health interest (submitted via our contact form)

3. How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used to:

Juvenis Medical LLC is a healthcare provider. Protected Health Information (PHI) collected in the course of treatment is governed by our HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices, available upon request. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties under any circumstances.

We use cookies to support analytics and site functionality. You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site features.

8. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
