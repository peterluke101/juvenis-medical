Services
Juvenis Medical offers peptide therapy, testosterone replacement therapy (TRT), bioidentical hormone replacement therapy (BHRT), medical weight loss including GLP-1 therapy, and men's health. All care plans are individualized based on your lab results — not templates.
Yes. BHRT and peptide therapy are both available for women. Many female patients come seeking support for perimenopause, menopause, thyroid function, and general hormone balance.
Testosterone replacement therapy (TRT) directly supplements testosterone levels and is a controlled substance regulated by state law. Peptide therapy uses naturally occurring signaling molecules that work with the body's own processes — supporting growth hormone release, recovery, and cellular function. Peptides are non-controlled and available nationwide via telehealth. Which approach is appropriate for you is a clinical determination made by Dr. Paul based on your labs and goals.
All medications at Juvenis Medical are sourced exclusively from licensed, accredited compounding pharmacies. Dr. Paul does not prescribe from unregulated or overseas sources. Suitability and safety are assessed individually during your evaluation.
The Process
