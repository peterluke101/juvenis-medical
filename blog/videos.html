Peptide Therapy

How to Self-Inject a Peptide — Step-by-Step with Dr. Paul

Best for: Patients starting BPC-157, Sermorelin, Ipamorelin/CJC-1295, or other subcutaneous peptides

Dr. Paul demonstrates the full process of preparing and self-administering a subcutaneous peptide injection at home — from reconstituting the vial to selecting the injection site to safely disposing of the needle. Watch this before your first dose.

What you'll see in this video
  1. Supplies checklist and sterile setup
  2. Reconstituting the peptide with bacteriostatic water
  3. Drawing the prescribed dose into the syringe
  4. Choosing and prepping the subcutaneous injection site
  5. Performing the injection
  6. Safe needle disposal and storage of remaining medication
Hormones & TRT

How to Self-Administer TRT — Full Demonstration with Dr. Paul

Best for: Patients on weekly testosterone cypionate or enanthate intramuscular protocols

A complete walkthrough of weekly testosterone replacement therapy injections — drawing the correct dose, choosing between intramuscular sites (deltoid, glute, quad), proper injection technique, and what to do if you miss a dose. Recommended viewing before your first self-administered injection.

What you'll see in this video
  1. Weekly TRT supplies and dose verification
  2. Drawing testosterone cypionate / enanthate from the vial
  3. Switching to the injection needle and removing air
  4. Intramuscular site rotation: deltoid, glute, and quad
  5. Injection technique and aspiration guidance
  6. What to do if you miss a dose, and when to call the office
