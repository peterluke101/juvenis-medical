# Dr. Paul — Social Content System

Same approach as the website: framework is built, you drop content in on top.

---

## How It Works

1. Pete picks 2 blog topics (1 men's, 1 women's)
2. Ares writes the blog + all 14 social posts from templates
3. Pete reviews → Dr. Paul approves → schedule and publish

One conversation. Everything generated. No starting from scratch.

---

## Folder Structure

```
social/
  templates/          — reusable post templates for every format
  2026-05/            — May 2026 content
    blog-1/           — men's topic
    blog-2/           — women's topic
  2026-06/            — June 2026 content (same structure)
  ...
```

Each monthly folder contains:
- `calendar.md` — full posting schedule with dates
- `blog-1/` and `blog-2/` — all assets for each blog post
  - `blog-post.md` — full blog draft
  - `tiktok-script.md` — 30-60 sec video script
  - `instagram-1.md` — graphic card caption
  - `instagram-2.md` — educational caption post
  - `facebook-1.md` — blog share post
  - `facebook-2.md` — standalone tip/question post
  - `google-business.md` — Google Business post
  - `linkedin.md` — LinkedIn post

---

## Monthly Trigger

At the start of each month Pete says:
"Topic 1: [men's topic]. Topic 2: [women's topic]."

Ares generates everything. Pete reviews. Done.
